What we stand for

  • GMO and Cruelty-free products - All our products are free from genetic manipulation and have been produced with respect to animals.
  • Fair wages - All our imported products are from a single source and the farmer is given a fair price for his crops.
  • Eco-friendliness - all our wrappings are either made of recycled paper or recycled plastic.

I have tested this website with: Chrome, Internet Explorer and Microsoft Edge. Internet explorer showed the greatest difference compared to the other two. The content seemed to be shifted slightly to the right, especially when there was a background-image used in css. Furthermore, internet explorer deleted the description labels from the form when you clicked on it, to allow you to type. The others didn't do that. I tested the print style by pressing ctrl+p in each browser and looked at the preview. The preview was consistent with how I set the print style properties with the page-break attribute.
